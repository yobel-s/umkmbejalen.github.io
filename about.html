About UMKM Desa Bejalen

UMKM Desa Bejalen is an initiative to empower the local community through small and medium-sized businesses. Our goal is to enhance the village economy by providing information and support for local businesses.

Vision and Mission

  • Support small and medium businesses to grow and prosper.
  • Provide a platform for business owners to showcase their work.
  • Boost the economy of Desa Bejalen through the development of UMKM.
